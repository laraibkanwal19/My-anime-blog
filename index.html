From Pencil to Screen: How I Turn My Sketches into Digital Art

I often start my anime art with pencil sketches on paper. But to bring them to life, I digitize them and add color and detail. In this blog post, I‚Äôll show you my step-by-step process from paper to screen.

Step 1: Sketching with Pencil

I start with a light pencil sketch in my notebook. I focus on the pose and face ‚Äî no details yet. I keep it simple and clean so it‚Äôs easy to trace later.

Step 2: Scanning or Taking a Photo

I use my phone to take a clear photo or scan the drawing. Then, I transfer it to my laptop or tablet. Make sure the lighting is good so the lines are visible.

Step 3: Tracing and Line Art

I open the image in MediBang or Krita and reduce the opacity. Then I use a new layer to trace the final line art using a digital brush. This step really cleans up the drawing.

Step 4: Coloring and Shading

Once the line art is done, I add base colors, then shadows and highlights. I experiment with different color palettes depending on the mood of the drawing.

It‚Äôs amazing to watch a simple paper sketch turn into a colorful digital illustration. This process lets me combine traditional and digital art skills!
